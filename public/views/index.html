Every day, we do things that influence our happiness. Even though we might be able to make generalizations about how these things impact us, the truth is that everybody is different! In this day and age where everybody is telling you what will make you happy, this site offers the opportunity for YOU to learn what makes YOU happy. It is my hope that you will learn things about yourself that you didn't even know. What I want you to do is to visit this website every evening and do the following:

 * Select several (~5+) activities you performed.
* Fill out the Physical, Mental & Psychological Wellness Survey below.
* That's it! We do the number crunching for you. All you have to do is wait to begin seeing the trends - how the things you do affect the way you feel. The longer you use this site, the better it will work.

 And, if you forget a day, don't worry! You can go back in time and create new entries, or make modifications to existing entries.
Selected Activities

Please rate your state of wellbeing in the following metrics:

Mental: Think about how your 'head' feels. Think about how well your brain is working. Are you sharp as a tack today? Is your short term memory working especially well? Have you been in a fog?
Physical: How your body feels. Does your knee hurt? Did you jump out of bed feeling like a spring chicken today?
Psychological: Think about how your 'heart' feels. Are you happy? Are you sad? Do you feel like jumping for joy or crawling into a corner?
